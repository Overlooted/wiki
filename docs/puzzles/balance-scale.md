# Balance Scale

Place the correct weight on a scale pan to balance it and open the gate.

## Components

| Component | Role |
|---|---|
| `ScalePanMono` | Measures total weight of objects on the pan |
| `BalanceDoorMono` | Opens when both pans are within balance tolerance |

## Setup

1. Place the **Balance Scale** prefab
2. Provide weight spheres with known mass values
3. Adjust the target weight on `BalanceDoorMono`
